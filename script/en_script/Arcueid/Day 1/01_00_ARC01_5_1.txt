I quietly follow her.
I lived in this house seven years ago, when I was a child. Although I remember some parts of it, my memory is too vague to make sense of it.
I took a single deep breath and stepped into my old home.

"Oh. I'm sorry, I took the wrong turn."
Whether she knew I was nervous or not, the girl turned around and bowed politely.

"Welcome home, Shiki-san.
I look forward to working for you."
...The girl's greeting really was a flowery smile.
Not being able to say a word in reply, I followed her without hesitation.
