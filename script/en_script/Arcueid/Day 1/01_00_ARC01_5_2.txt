I decided to stop her and ask for her name.
I felt bad calling her "maid".
<Page2>
"Excuse me, may I ask your name?
I just got here, so I don't really know what's going on at home."
I call out to her.
The girl in the kimono looked at me in surprise, then said, "Kohaku."
<Page3>
"My name is Kohaku. I work for the head of the Tohno family in this mansion."
<Page4>
Smiling, the girl, Kohaku, bowed.
The gesture was gentle, like a flower opening from a bud.
